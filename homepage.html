Travel Destinations

Palawan (El Nido)

A tropical paradise with breathtaking lagoons and pristine beaches, perfect for island hopping.

Boracay

Famous for its powdery white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife, a must-visit for relaxation and fun.

Banaue Rice Terraces

Marvel at the ancient engineering of the Ifugao, featuring stunning terraces that cascade down the mountains.

Chocolate Hills (Bohol)

Unique geological formations that turn brown in the dry season, resembling chocolate mounds.

Vigan

UNESCO World Heritage site known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and charming cobblestone streets.

Kawasan Falls (Cebu)

Dive into turquoise waters and swim beneath stunning waterfalls in a tropical paradise.

Siargao

Surf the legendary Cloud 9 waves or unwind on pristine beaches in this surfer’s haven.

Coron (Palawan)

Explore world-class wreck diving sites and stunning lagoons, ideal for adventure seekers.

Intramuros (Manila)

Step back in time in the historic walled city where Spanish colonial history comes alive.

Taal Volcano (Batangas)

One of the most picturesque volcanoes, surrounded by a stunning lake and scenic views.

Batanes

Experience breathtaking landscapes and a unique culture in the northernmost islands of the Philippines.

Mayon Volcano (Albay)

Famous for its perfect cone shape, Mayon offers breathtaking views and thrilling hikes.

Pagsanjan Falls (Laguna)

Experience a thrilling boat ride to the stunning waterfalls, surrounded by lush greenery.

Mount Pulag

Hike to the highest peak in Luzon for a breathtaking sea of clouds at sunrise.

Dumaguete (Siquijor)

Known for its laid-back vibe, beautiful beaches, and stunning diving spots.

Samar Island Natural Park

Explore vast wilderness, caves, and pristine rivers in one of the country’s largest protected areas.

Hanging Coffins (Sagada)

Witness the unique burial tradition of the Igorot people, suspended in cliffs.

Camiguin

Home to the ‘Island Born of Fire,’ it boasts hot springs, waterfalls, and the famous White Island sandbar.

Venice Grand Canal (Taguig)

Experience a slice of Italy in the heart of Taguig, where picturesque canals and charming architecture create a romantic escape.

Enchanted River (Surigao del Sur)

A mystical blue river surrounded by lush forests, perfect for swimming and relaxation.
